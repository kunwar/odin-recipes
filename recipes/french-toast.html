French Toast

Description

It's easier than you think to make restaurant-quality French toast in the comfort of your own kitchen â€“ you just need a skillet, a few staple ingredients, and a good recipe. That's where we come in!

The best breads for French toast are brioche, sourdough, French bread, or challah. These varieties are dense and sturdy enough to handle total saturation in the wet, milky, egg custard without falling apart. However, in a pinch, any thick-sliced white bread will do.

Ingredients

  • White bread
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Vanilla and cinnamon
  • Salt
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together in a shallow bowl.
  3. Lightly butter a griddle or skillet and heat over medium-high heat.
  4. Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides.
  5. Transfer to the hot skillet and cook until golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side.
  6. Serve hot.
